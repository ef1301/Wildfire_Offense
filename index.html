"Over the past 10 years,
There were an average of 67,000 wildfires annually
And an average of 7.0 million acres burned annually."

Austrailia is being ravaged by the worst wildfires in decades since September 2019.

Common causes of bushfires include
  • lightning
  • arcing from overhead power lines
  • arson
  • accidental ignition in the course of agricultural clearing
  • grinding and welding activities
  • campfires
  • cigarettes and dropped matches
